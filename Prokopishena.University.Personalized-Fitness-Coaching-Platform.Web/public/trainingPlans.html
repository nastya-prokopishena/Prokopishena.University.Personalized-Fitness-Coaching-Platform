    Frequently Asked Questions

    How can I request training for a specific trainer?

    To request training for a particular trainer, navigate to their profile and click on the "Request Training" button.

    How do I view information about clients for a specific trainer?

    To view client information for a particular trainer, go to the trainer's profile page and click on the "View Clients" button.

    What payment methods are accepted?

    We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. You can choose the most convenient option during the checkout process.

    Is there a free trial available?

    Yes, we offer a free trial period for new users. During this time, you can explore our platform and its features before committing to a subscription.

    How can I cancel my subscription?

    To cancel your subscription, please log in to your account, go to the settings or subscription page, and follow the instructions provided.

    Do you offer personalized training plans?

    Yes, we provide personalized training plans tailored to your fitness goals, preferences, and level of experience.

    Troubleshooting Guide

    Problem: Unable to find a client or trainer.

    Ensure that you have entered the correct user ID. If the issue persists, please refresh the page.

    Problem: Information about a client or trainer is not displaying.

    Check your internet connection and try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, please contact support.

    Problem: Unable to submit a training request.

    Make sure all required fields are filled out correctly. If the problem persists, try clearing your browser's cache and cookies or using a different browser.

    Problem: Video tutorials are not playing.

    Check your internet connection and ensure that your browser supports the video format. You may also try updating your browser or using a different device.

    Problem: Unable to access my account.

    Make sure you are using the correct email and password combination. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" option to reset it.

    Problem: Error messages during checkout.

    Double-check your payment information and ensure that your billing address matches the one associated with your payment method. If the problem persists, try using a different payment method or contact your bank.
